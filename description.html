Description

You have two choices. You can create password only with numbers or numbers with letters. Letters can be small and big.
Before you start you have to enter numeric value to determine the length of your password. In case you forget to do this, the app automatically gives length of password 10 characters. After you determined the length you can generate password by clicking the button "Generate....". If you are not happy of the result you can click the button again to regenerate a new one. You can do this as many times as you want. Also you can clear the password's textbox by clicking the button "Clear" and also copy the generated password by clicking "Copy". Thank you for your patience and goodbye!
